Hi! I'm Himanshu Sahni.

And I'm currently on the job hunt! I am a PhD student in Reinforcement Learning with Dr. Charles Isbell at Georgia Tech, graduating in early 2021. My thesis work is on speeding up reinforcement learning in particularly challenging environments, such as visual navigation with sparse rewards, or partially observable visual domains. I am also interested in heirarchical learning and compositionality. During my PhD, I have published at top ML conferences such as NeurIPS, ICML and AAMAS. I have also interned at Microsoft Research, Tesla Autopilot, Facebook and Offworld Inc. I graduated with a B. Tech. from IIT Delhi in 2011.
