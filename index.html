Hi, I'm Himanshu!

I am a PhD student in Reinforcement Learning and I'm currently on the job hunt! My thesis work is on speeding up reinforcement learning in particularly challenging environments, such as visual navigation with sparse rewards, or partially observable visual domains. I am also interested in heirarchical learning and compositionality. During my PhD, I have published at top ML conferences such as NeurIPS, ICML and AAMAS. I have also interned at Microsoft Research, Tesla Autopilot, Facebook and Offworld Inc. My thesis advisor is Dr. Charles Isbell. I graduated with a B. Tech. from IIT Delhi in 2011.
